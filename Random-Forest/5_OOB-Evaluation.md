**Full Form of OOB :** Out of Bag 
OOB validation is used for Testing the Model with the Out of bag Rows as a test sample 

 **Explanation :**
 jab Hum random forest mein data ki sampling with replacement karte hai tab kuch aise rows Reh jaate hai Jo kabhi Decision Trees tak nhi pahuch pate 
 In Rows ko OOB rows kaha jata hai 
 **Ex :** Maanlo 100 rows ka dataset hai aap 50-50 rows sab base models ko de rhe ho ab kyuki aap row sampling with replacement kar rhe ho ho skta hai ki kkuch rows Multiple time ja rhi ho jiski wajah kuch row nhi ja pa rhi 

**Note :** its mathematical proven or statistical Proven that 37% of rows in dataset is OOB Rows
