
****
**Bias :** Jab aapka machine learning Model Training Data pe Acche result nhi de pata 
**Variance :** Jab aapka Machine learning Model Test Data pe Acche result Nhi De pata 
**relationship :** Bias is inversely proportional to Variance 

In dono ke basis Machine learning Algo Classify hai Jaise : 
1. **LBHV :** Low Bais High Variance Eg. Fully Grown Decision Tree , SVM,KNN
2. **HBLV :** High Bais Low Variance Eg. Linear regression 

Hum LBLV Milna sabse Mushkil hota hai 
Bias Means Underfitting 
Variance means Overfitting 

You always Need Low Bias and Low Variance Model 
Random Forest hume Madad karta hai Aise model banane mein Jisme Hum Low bias High Variance Model daalte Hai Hum Low bias Low variance model Milta hai
