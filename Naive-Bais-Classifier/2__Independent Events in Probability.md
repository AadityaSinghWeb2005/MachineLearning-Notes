**Independant Events**

- Agar Kuch Events simoultanously ho rhe hai toh Woh ek dusre ke results ko effect nhi karenge 
- **Example :** Agar Hum 3 baar Toss kare aur har bar head aaye toh 4th baar Kya aaenga obviously Koi effect nhi padega 0.5 hi probability hi rahega 
- **Mathematical form :** P(A intersection B) = P(A) * P(B)
- Agar yeh mathematical Condition True ho rhi hai toh Mtlb events Independants Hai

**Mutually Exclusive Events**

-  Two Events A and B exclusive hoge jab P(A intersection B) = 0 
- **Example :** Head aur Tail Saath mein nhi aaskte Sirf Ek hi aaskta hai
