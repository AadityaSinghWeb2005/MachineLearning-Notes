1. **Types of Base Model :** Yeh Dono hi ensemble Learning ke Type hai Aur Ensemble learning ka Goal hota hai Low bias and Low variance Acheive Karna 
	**Bagging :** isme unmodels ka Conversion hota hai Jinka Low bias High Variance hota hai Example Fully Grown Decision Tree yeh overfitting ka Case hai 
	**Boosting :** Isme Un Models ka conversion hota hai jinka High bias And Low Variance hota hai. Inhe Weak Learners kehte hai ex decision Stump ( decision Tree with max depth = 1)
****
**Types of Learning (parallel vs Sequential)**
- **Bagging :** isme dataset ko subsets mein tod ke randomly train kiya hai Jata hai Multiple models independently train Hota hai 
- **Boosting :** Features Sequential Learning. Models are trained one after another. Each subsequent model attempts to correct the errors made by the previous model,passing on information about those mistakes to the next learner 

****
**Weightage of Base Models** 
- **Bagging :** Every Base Model has Equal Weightage 
- **Boosting :** Models have different weightages
